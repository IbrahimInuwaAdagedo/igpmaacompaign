Campaign Launch

The official campaign of IGP Mohammad Abubakar Adamu has commenced with a renewed call for peace, unity, and development across Nigeria. The event witnessed community leaders, youth representatives, and security stakeholders reaffirming their support for a safer and more united nation.

Youth Engagement Program

As part of his vision for youth empowerment, IGP Adamu launched an initiative providing mentorship, leadership training, and skill acquisition opportunities for young Nigerians, ensuring their active participation in national development.
